About Us

Neyveli Lignite Corporation India Limited (NLCIL) is a prominent player in the energy sector of India, founded in 1956. It operates lignite mines and a series of power plants, harnessing the resources of lignite for sustainable energy generation. The corporation is headquartered in Neyveli, Tamil Nadu, and plays a significant role in contributing to India's energy security with eco-friendly initiatives.

With a mission to promote sustainable development, NLCIL is focused on power generation and actively engages in solar power projects and various initiatives contributing to environmental sustainability.

Our Services

NLCIL offers a diverse range of services that include:

  • Lignite Mining: Operating and maintaining lignite mines ensuring efficient and sustainable extraction.
  • Power Generation: Producing energy through thermal and renewable energy sources such as solar power.
  • Environmental Management: Commitment to sustainable practices, including afforestation and community development.
  • Educational Services: Supporting educational programs and skill development initiatives for local communities.
